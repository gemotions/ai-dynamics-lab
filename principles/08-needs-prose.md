There are six Need energies.

Body points down. Its vector is [0.0, -1.0, 0.0].

Head points up. Its vector is [0.0, 1.0, 0.0].

Group points back. Its vector is [0.0, 0.0, -1.0].

Self points front. Its vector is [0.0, 0.0, 1.0].

Outer points right. Its vector is [1.0, 0.0, 0.0].

Inner points left. Its vector is [-1.0, 0.0, 0.0].

Each Need is one of six directions on the sphere.

Body and Head are opposite. Group and Self are opposite. Outer and Inner are opposite.
